How It Works

Simple, transparent, and effective grievance redressal mechanism

1

Register Account

Create your secure account with citizenship details and contact information for verified access to services.

2

Lodge Complaint

Submit detailed complaints through our smart system that automatically categorizes and routes to appropriate authorities.

3

AI Classification

Advanced AI analyzes your complaint and intelligently routes it to Federal, Provincial, or Local government bodies.

4

Real-time Tracking

Track your complaint status in real-time with notifications at every stage from registration to resolution.

5

Department Collaboration

Multiple departments can collaborate on complex issues with coordinated updates and action plans.

6

Resolution & Feedback

Receive resolution updates and provide feedback on service quality to help us improve continuously.
