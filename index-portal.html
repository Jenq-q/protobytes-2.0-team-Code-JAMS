How It Works

Simple, transparent, and effective grievance redressal mechanism powered by AI

1

Register with 2FA

Create your secure account with citizenship details, phone verification via OTP, and complete address including house number for verified access.

2

Lodge Complaint

Submit detailed complaints with categories including corruption & bribery. Our smart system automatically categorizes and routes to appropriate authorities.

3

AI Classification

Advanced AI analyzes your complaint and intelligently routes it to Federal, Provincial, or Local government bodies based on jurisdiction and severity.

4

Real-time Updates

Receive instant notifications as your complaint moves through the system. Track progress with detailed timeline and status updates at every step.

5

Department Action

Relevant department officials review and take action. For critical issues, cases may be escalated to PM Office, CIAA, or other high authorities.

6

Resolution & Feedback

Once resolved, provide feedback on your experience. Your input helps improve the system and holds authorities accountable for quality service.

Nepal's Three-Tier Government System

Understanding how your complaints reach the right authorities

Federal Government

National-level governance including defense, foreign affairs, major infrastructure, and cross-provincial matters. Handles issues of national importance and high-priority cases.

Provincial Government

Seven provinces managing regional development, provincial roads, agriculture, tourism, and provincial public services across their jurisdictions.

Local Government

753 local units (Municipalities & Rural Municipalities) handling local infrastructure, sanitation, basic services, and community-level governance.
